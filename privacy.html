Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Crewallet is designed to help cruise ship crew members track earnings such as tips, incentives, contracts, and pay periods.

No Data Collection

Crewallet does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal data.

Local Storage Only

All data entered into the app is stored locally on the user's device. This includes:

  • Contracts
  • Tips entries
  • Incentives
  • Pay periods
  • User preferences

No Tracking

Crewallet does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking tools.

Backups

The app allows users to manually export backups of their data. These backups are stored only where the user chooses.
