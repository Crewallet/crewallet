Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Crewallet is designed to help onboard crew members keep contracts, tips, incentives, expenses, pay periods and financial history organized in one place.

No Data Collection

Crewallet does not collect, store, transmit or sell personal data to the developer.

Local Storage Only

Data entered into Crewallet is stored locally on the user’s device. Depending on how the app is used, this may include:

  • Contracts and contract details
  • Daily tips entries
  • Incentives and bonuses
  • Pay period information and overrides
  • Expense categories and expense entries
  • Goals and preferences
  • Appearance and currency settings

No Tracking

Crewallet does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs or third-party tracking tools.

Backups and Exports

Crewallet allows users to manually export backups and PDF reports. These files are created only when the user chooses to do so and are stored only in the location selected by the user.

No Account Required

Crewallet does not require account creation or sign-in to use the app.
