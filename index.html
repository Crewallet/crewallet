Crewallet Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Overview

Crewallet is designed to help cruise ship crew members track their earnings during contracts.

Your privacy is extremely important. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

Crewallet does not collect any personal information.

No analytics, tracking systems, or external servers are used.

Local Data Storage

All data entered in the app (tips, incentives, contracts, and pay periods) is stored locally on the user's device.

The developer cannot access or retrieve this data.

Backups

The app allows users to manually export backups of their data. These backups are stored only where the user chooses to save them.

Third-Party Services

Crewallet does not integrate with any third-party services.
