Crewallet Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Crewallet is designed to help cruise ship crew members track earnings such as tips and incentives during their contracts.

No Data Collection

Crewallet does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Local Storage Only

All data entered into the app is stored locally on the user’s device. This includes:

  • Contracts
  • Tips entries
  • Incentives
  • Pay periods
  • User preferences

No data is sent to external servers.

No Tracking

Crewallet does not use analytics, tracking systems, advertising SDKs, or third-party data collection services.

Data Control

Users have full control over their data. Data can be exported using the in-app backup feature.
